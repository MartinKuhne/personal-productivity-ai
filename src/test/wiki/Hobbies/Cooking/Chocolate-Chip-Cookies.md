# Chocolate Chip Cookies

The secret to these cookies is browning the butter first.

## Ingredients
| Ingredient | Amount |
|------------|--------|
| Butter | 1 cup (browned) |
| Brown Sugar| 1 cup |
| White Sugar| 0.5 cup |
| Flour | 2.25 cups |
| Choc Chips | 2 cups |

## Steps
1. Brown the butter and let it cool slightly.
2. Mix in sugars, then eggs and vanilla.
3. Fold in dry ingredients.
4. Chill dough for 30 minutes.
5. Bake at 350°F for 10-12 minutes.
