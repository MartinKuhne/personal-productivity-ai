# Classic Sourdough Bread

Baking sourdough requires patience, but the results are incredibly rewarding.

## Ingredients
* 500g Bread Flour
* 350g Water (room temperature)
* 100g Active Sourdough Starter
* 10g Salt

## Instructions

1. **Autolyse**: Mix flour and water. Let sit for 1 hour.
2. **Add Starter & Salt**: Dimple the starter and salt into the dough.
3. **Bulk Fermentation**: Perform stretch and folds every 30 minutes for 2 hours. Let rest until doubled.
4. **Shaping**: Shape the dough into a boule and place in a proofing basket.
5. **Cold Retard**: Place in the fridge overnight.
6. **Baking**: 
    - Preheat oven to 450°F (230°C) with Dutch oven inside.
    - Score the dough and bake covered for 20 minutes.
    - Remove lid and bake for another 25 minutes.
