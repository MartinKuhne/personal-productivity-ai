# Japan Spring Trip 🌸

We are planning a two-week trip to Japan to catch the cherry blossoms!

## Places to Visit

### Tokyo
- **Shibuya Crossing**: See the famous scramble.
- **Akihabara**: Electronics and anime culture.
- **Senso-ji Temple**: Historic temple in Asakusa.

### Kyoto
- **Fushimi Inari Taisha**: The iconic thousands of torii gates.
- **Kinkaku-ji**: The Golden Pavilion.
