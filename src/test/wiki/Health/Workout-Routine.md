# Push / Pull / Legs Split

This is the routine I follow to build strength and maintain overall fitness.

## Schedule

* **Monday**: Push (Chest, Shoulders, Triceps)
* **Tuesday**: Pull (Back, Biceps)
* **Wednesday**: Rest or Active Recovery
* **Thursday**: Legs (Quads, Hamstrings, Calves)
* **Friday**: Upper Body Mix
* **Weekend**: Hiking or cycling

## Push Day Exercises
1. Barbell Bench Press: 4 sets of 8-10 reps
2. Overhead Press: 3 sets of 10 reps
3. Incline Dumbbell Press: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
4. Tricep Pushdowns: 3 sets of 15 reps
5. Lateral Raises: 4 sets of 15-20 reps
