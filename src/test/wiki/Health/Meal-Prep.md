# Meal Prep

Planning meals for the work week.

## Monday - Wednesday
* **Breakfast**: Oatmeal with whey protein and berries.
* **Lunch**: Chicken breast with quinoa and roasted broccoli.
* **Dinner**: Salmon with sweet potato and asparagus.

## Thursday - Friday
* **Breakfast**: Scrambled eggs with spinach and whole wheat toast.
* **Lunch**: Turkey chili with brown rice.
* **Dinner**: Lean beef stir-fry with mixed vegetables.
